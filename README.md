### Internship
Future Interns – Data Science & Analytics

### Task
Task 1: Business Sales Performance Analytics

## Project Overview
This project analyzes the Sample Superstore dataset using Microsoft Power BI. The dashboard provides insights into sales trends, profitability, product categories, customer segments, and regional performance.

## Dashboard Features
- Total Sales KPI
- Total Profit KPI
- Total Orders KPI
- Total Quantity Sold KPI
- Sales Trend Over Time
- Sales by Category
- Top 10 Products by Sales
- Profit by Region
- Sales by Segment
- Interactive Filters (Region, Category, Order Date)

## Business Insights
- Total Sales: 2.30M
- Total Profit: 286.40K
- Total Orders: 5K
- Total Quantity Sold: 38K
- Technology category generated the highest sales.
- West region recorded the highest profit.
- Consumer segment contributed the largest share of sales.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Power BI
- Microsoft Excel
